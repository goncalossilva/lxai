Lisbon AI started as a notsoquiet group chat of hackers reminiscing about LXJS and wondering why there wasnt a European, builderfirst AI gathering. We fixed that.

Community run

Organised by people who ship opensource for a living. Volunteer based.

Singletrack talks, multitrack fun

You never miss a talk, but you choose your dinners, workshops, and hackathon project.

Open-source

Every speaker presents in code; every talk is live streamed.

Ethical by design

Code of Conduct, diverse curation, and scholarships baked in.
